KEYBINDS

QWASE - edit tile
Arrows - cycle grid
- and = to decrement or increment grid height
[ and ] to decrement or increment grid width
\ to step the interpreter
alt right-click to copy grid as image
middle-click to copy tile contents

right-click drag to select area
- CTRL-C to copy
- CTRL-X to cut
- CTRL-V to paste at upper left corner of selection
- BACKSPACE to fill selection
- DEL to fill selection with empty
